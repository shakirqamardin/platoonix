1. Nature of service

Platoonix is a discovery platform connecting loaders with hauliers for backhaul transport opportunities.

We are not:

  • A transport operator or haulage company
  • A freight forwarder
  • An employer or employment agency
  • A price-setting service

We are:

  • A marketplace platform connecting independent businesses
  • A location-based discovery service (25-mile corridor matching)
  • A payment processor (secure escrow only)
  • A verification service (insurance, DVLA checks)

All users operate as independent contractors running their own businesses. Platoonix does not employ users, set prices, assign jobs, or control how transport services are performed.

2. Discovery service

How the platform works

  1. Loaders post transport requirements with their budget.
  2. Hauliers search using their current location and destination.
  3. The platform shows loads within 25 miles of the haulier’s route (corridor matching).
  4. Hauliers choose which loads suit their business.
  5. The platform facilitates booking and payment (escrow).

Haulier autonomy

Hauliers have freedom to: enter any route they choose; view available opportunities; accept or decline any load; negotiate with loaders; and arrange delivery independently, subject to any agreement they reach with the loader.

Loader autonomy

Loaders have freedom to: set their own budgets; define their requirements; choose which hauliers to work with; and cancel in line with the cancellation policy below.

The platform matches users based on location proximity and posted criteria. Business decisions (pricing beyond the posted budget where agreed, timing, and how work is performed) remain with users.

6. Pricing and payment

Loaders set their own budget for each load. The platform may provide optional industry guidance only; it does not set the price. Final pricing for the posted load is determined by the loader (subject to any separate agreement they reach with a haulier).

Hauliers choose which loads to accept based on the offered budget. Hauliers may negotiate directly with loaders. Any amount agreed between users beyond or instead of the posted budget is a matter for those parties.

Platform fees

  • Loader booking fee: £5 minimum or 2% of the load budget (whichever is greater), as shown in the app at the time the charge applies.
  • Haulier service fee: 8% of the accepted load budget (as shown in the app).

The 8% service fee covers: location-based discovery (25-mile corridor search); payment processing (secure escrow); insurance and DVLA verification; communication tools; and platform maintenance. Fees are for discovery and facilitation services only.

The platform does not take commission on amounts users negotiate between themselves outside the fee structure described in the app. If you agree a different price with another user, platform fees still apply as calculated on the basis set out in the product and these terms (typically on the accepted load budget recorded on the platform).

9. Cancellation policy

9.1 Loader cancellation

Loaders may cancel loads at any time before the scheduled pickup time with no financial penalty.

How to cancel

  • Open loads: click “Cancel” on the load in your dashboard
  • Matched loads: click “Cancel job” — the job is cancelled and the load reopened automatically
  • After pickup has started: contact support@platoonix.co.uk immediately

What happens when you cancel

  • You receive a full refund of your payment where applicable
  • The haulier is notified immediately
  • If the load was matched, it is reopened for other hauliers to accept
  • The cancellation is recorded on your account profile

Frequent cancellations

While there is no financial penalty for cancellation, we track cancellation rates to maintain platform quality. Loaders with high cancellation rates (for example above 20%) may:

  • Receive lower priority in haulier searches
  • Have their cancellation rate displayed on their profile
  • Be contacted by our support team to discuss reliability concerns
  • Face account restrictions if the pattern continues

9.2 Haulier cancellation

Hauliers may cancel accepted jobs at any time before pickup with no financial penalty.

How to cancel

  • Click “Cancel” on the job in your dashboard
  • The job is cancelled and the load reopened for other hauliers
  • After collection has started: contact support@platoonix.co.uk immediately

What happens when you cancel

  • The loader is notified immediately
  • The load is automatically reopened for other hauliers
  • The loader receives a full refund where a payment was taken
  • The cancellation is recorded on your account profile

Frequent cancellations

Hauliers with high cancellation rates (for example above 15%) may:

  • Have their cancellation rate displayed on their profile
  • Receive fewer load recommendations
  • Be contacted by our support team
  • Face account restrictions if reliability does not improve

9.3 Reputation system

Both loaders and hauliers can see each other’s:

  • Star rating (1–5 stars based on completed jobs)
  • Number of completed jobs
  • Cancellation rate (percentage of jobs or loads cancelled)
  • Reviews from other users

This transparency allows both parties to make informed decisions about who they work with.

9.4 No-show policy

If a haulier fails to arrive for pickup within 30 minutes of the scheduled time without prior communication:

  • The loader may report a no-show
  • The job is cancelled and the load reopened as emergency priority
  • The loader receives a full refund
  • The haulier’s account is flagged for review
  • Repeated no-shows may result in account suspension

9.5 Refund processing

All refunds are processed within 2–3 business days via the original payment method.

9.6 Dispute resolution

If you have a concern about a cancellation, contact support@platoonix.co.uk within 48 hours with details. Our team will review and respond within 2 business days.

9.7 After collection and other disputes

After collection has begun: If the loader cancels after the haulier has collected the goods, the loader must pay the full agreed amount unless otherwise agreed. The haulier will complete delivery to the original destination unless alternative arrangements are mutually agreed.

If the haulier cancels after collection, they must return the goods to the pickup location at their own expense where applicable. The loader may receive a full refund of eligible fees.

Non-delivery or delays: If goods are not delivered within a reasonable time after collection, or if delivery is significantly delayed without valid reason, payment may be withheld pending investigation.

Damaged or lost goods: Resolution is between parties and insurers where applicable; Platoonix is not liable for cargo (see Sections 5 and 6).

Rejected deliveries: Return and costs may be negotiated between parties.

General disputes: See Section 5 (Platform liability and user responsibilities).
