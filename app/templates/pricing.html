How pricing works

Platoonix is a discovery platform connecting loaders with hauliers for backhaul opportunities.

Included: smart matching

How it works

  1. Best match first — vehicle type, capacity, trailer, and required equipment
  2. Then by distance — road miles to pickup; closer first within the same tier
  3. Fair — no paid priority in organic search; deterministic ordering

Your benefits

  • ✓Compatible loads only — less wasted time
  • ✓Fewer empty miles
  • ✓Fair visibility (not pay-to-rank)
  • ✓Faster, more relevant matches
Smart matching is included for all users — not a paid upsell. See Terms (section 11) and API docs.

Why hybrid pricing?

Our booking fee covers:

  • Payment processing (Stripe fees ~{{ "%.0f"|format(loader_fee_percent_of_load) }}%)
  • Platform listing
  • Match notifications

The minimum £{{ "%.2f"|format(loader_min_fee_gbp) }} keeps smaller loads viable for us to run. On larger loads, {{ "%.0f"|format(loader_fee_percent_of_load) }}% scales fairly.
